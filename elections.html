Ben Aubin

Running For:

  1. Corporate
  2. Finance
Platform:
As ACM corporate officer, I plan to continue my track record of bringing numerous new sponsors to ACM and running unique events that take advantage of our spot in Austin's tech ecosystem. Over the past year, I started two new sponsored event series: LiveOak connections and Hack Night with Z Fellows. I've brought in 3 new sponsors, increased revenue per sponsor by revamping our sponsorship tiers and marketing, and connected ACM with numerous local startups. I believe great corporate events create unique events for students: companies want to send their engineers to participate in hackathons with our community, investors want first preference if you're starting a startup, and many consumer-focused organizations like Cruise/BeReal seek to expand their customer base by offering free trials and perks to student orgs. As corporate officer, I'd help ACM participate in these conversations and design events that improve the student experience and help ACM fund its activities. I have a track record of bringing in great sponsors to ACM and would love to continue this in the upcoming academic year. As well, I've been significantly involved in the effort to represent CS organizations so that the org offices remain a common resource. A primary goal of my term will be to strength inter-org collaboration and improve our relationship with the CS department and CNS.
