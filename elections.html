2018 - 2019 Texas Association for Computing Machinery Senior Officer Elections

If you are interested in running for a Senior officer position, please fill out this form by Monday, April 9th, at 11:59PM.
The Duties and Expectations of each Officer Position can be found here.
The elections will be held on Thursday, April 12th, at 7:00pm in GDC 5.302.
You can check out 2017-2018 Candidates' last year's platforms here if you want to know what to expect.

If you have any questions, please email hr@texasacm.org.

Officer Requirements

  • • Must have at least a 2.75 GPA
  • • Corporate Officers must be past Entry Level (completed CS 312, CS 314, CS 311, and any required Calculus for your degree)
  • • Must be enrolled at UT and be on the UT Austin campus for both semesters next year

Please keep in mind that being an ACM officer does require time commitments. If at any point you fail to meet expectations, you may be subject to removal from your position.
