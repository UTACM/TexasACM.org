2022 - 2023 Texas Association for Computing Machinery Senior Officer Elections

Check out our candidates for the 2022-2023 Officer Elections. All applications are closed unless you are running for Webmaster, Corporate, or Internal (if so please email hr@texasacm.org with your name, school year, and platform to be considered on a case-by-case basis.) Hope to see you at the elections on Thursday, April 21st, at 5 pm in GDC 1.304.

Macy Huang

Running For:

  1. Human Resources
  2. Corporate
Platform:
This year, I served as HR Senior Officer and I feel that it gave me a lot of insight into what makes events successful -- clear advertisement, a sense of purpose, and the proper incentives (food). I attended almost every ACM event this semester, and what you should take from that is not that I will do anything I can to postpone working on OS (this was a bit of a factor though), but that I have dedicated myself as an officer to making sure events run smoothly, whether that means offering feedback in biweekly officer meetings, talking to new people during events, or helping out behind the scenes to make sure everything is in place. Next year, I hope to continue to do the same while offering more ways for ACM members to Associate with each other, in Computing and non-computing related ways MMMM(achinery). This would include our existing mentorship program, but also initiatives I'd like to start, like club Brown Bags, so club members can meet new people on a one-on-one basis. What I've found most valuable about ACM in my time as a member over the last two years isn't the communal Nintendo Switch, microwave, nor the fridge in the office, but the really cool people within it, and as your HR Officer next year, I would love to highlight that and do what I can to integrate more people into the fold.
