About Us

The Association for Computing Machinery is a UT chapter of the national ACM, and exists to create a community among Computer Science students and anyone interested in technology. ACM holds events that serves its members or the community, and is focused on the advancement of computer science studies and research, furthering computing as a profession and a science.

Why Join?

Academic

Our mentorship program matches upperclassmen with incoming freshman, who can help out with the social, academic, and career aspects of college. The mentorship program aims to form tight-knit friends and connections. You can apply to be a mentor or mentee here.

Network / Professional

ACM also helps with Professional Networking. Our many General Meetings and Tech Talks allows members to meet company recruiters, learn about opportinuties, and learn how to write a good resume.

Social

Don't just work all the time, fun and friends are important too! Make friends by joining ACM, and have fun in our many social events such as Family Dinners, Retreat, and Game Nights!
